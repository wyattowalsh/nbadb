# Team Stats Endpoints

Six endpoints provide team-level data.

## CommonTeamYears

List of all teams with their years of activity.

- **Result sets**: `TeamYears`
- **Key columns**: `league_id`, `team_id`, `min_year`, `max_year`, `abbreviation`

## TeamInfoCommon

Current team information including record and rankings.

- **Result sets**: `TeamInfoCommon`, `TeamSeasonRanks`
- **Parameters**: `team_id` (required)
- **Key columns**: `team_city`, `team_name`, `team_abbreviation`, `team_conference`, `team_division`, `w`, `l`, `pct`, `conf_rank`, `div_rank`

## TeamDetails

Detailed team background including ownership and arena.

- **Result sets**: `TeamBackground`, `TeamHistory`, `TeamSocialSites`, `TeamAwardsChampionships`, `TeamAwardsConf`, `TeamAwardsDiv`, `TeamHof`, `TeamRetired`
- **Parameters**: `team_id` (required)
- **Key columns**: `abbreviation`, `nickname`, `yearfounded`, `city`, `arena`, `arenacapacity`, `owner`, `generalmanager`, `headcoach`

## CommonTeamRoster

Current and historical team rosters and coaching staff.

- **Result sets**: `CommonTeamRoster`, `Coaches`
- **Parameters**: `team_id` (required), `season` (required)
- **Key columns**: Player roster data (player_id, position, height, weight, birth_date, age, exp, school) and coaching staff data

## FranchiseHistory

Complete franchise history including relocations and name changes.

- **Result sets**: `FranchiseHistory`, `DefunctTeams`
- **Key columns**: `team_id`, `team_city`, `team_name`, `start_year`, `end_year`, `years`, `games`, `wins`, `losses`, `win_pct`, `po_appearances`, `div_titles`, `conf_titles`, `league_titles`

## TeamYearByYearStats

Year-by-year team statistics across all seasons.

- **Result sets**: `TeamStats`
- **Parameters**: `team_id` (required)
