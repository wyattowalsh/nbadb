# Player Stats Endpoints

Seven endpoints provide comprehensive player data.

## CommonAllPlayers

Master list of all NBA players (historical and current).

- **Result sets**: `CommonAllPlayers`
- **Key columns**: `person_id`, `display_first_last`, `roster_status`, `from_year`, `to_year`, `team_id`

## PlayerIndex

Detailed player index with biographical and current season stats.

- **Result sets**: `PlayerIndex`
- **Key columns**: `person_id`, `player_first_name`, `player_last_name`, `position`, `height`, `weight`, `college`, `country`, `draft_year`, `draft_round`, `draft_number`, `pts`, `reb`, `ast`

## CommonPlayerInfo

Detailed biographical and career information for a specific player.

- **Result sets**: `CommonPlayerInfo`, `PlayerHeadlineStats`, `AvailableSeasons`
- **Parameters**: `player_id` (required)
- **Key columns**: `birthdate`, `school`, `country`, `height`, `weight`, `season_exp`, `jersey`, `position`, `draft_year`, `draft_round`, `draft_number`

## PlayerCareerStats

Season-by-season career statistics across regular season, playoffs, and All-Star games.

- **Result sets**: `SeasonTotalsRegularSeason`, `SeasonTotalsPostSeason`, `SeasonTotalsAllStarSeason`, `CareerTotalsRegularSeason`, `CareerTotalsPostSeason`
- **Parameters**: `player_id` (required)
- **Key columns**: Full traditional stat line (gp, gs, min, fgm, fga, fg_pct, fg3m, fg3a, fg3_pct, ftm, fta, ft_pct, oreb, dreb, reb, ast, stl, blk, tov, pf, pts)

## PlayerAwards

All awards and honors received by a player.

- **Result sets**: `PlayerAwards`
- **Parameters**: `player_id` (required)
- **Key columns**: `description`, `all_nba_team_number`, `season`, `conference`, `type`

## PlayerDashboardByYearOverYear

Year-over-year statistical comparisons for a player.

- **Result sets**: `OverallPlayerDashboard`, `ByYearPlayerDashboard`
- **Parameters**: `player_id` (required), `season` (required)

## PlayerGameStreakFinder

Find player game streaks matching specific criteria.

- **Result sets**: `PlayerGameStreakFinderResults`
- **Parameters**: Various filter criteria
