# League Stats Endpoints

Eight endpoints provide league-wide statistical views.

## LeagueDashPlayerStats

League-wide player statistics dashboard with extensive filtering.

- **Result sets**: `LeagueDashPlayerStats`
- **Parameters**: `season` (required), `season_type`, `per_mode`, `measure_type`
- **Key columns**: Full traditional + advanced stat line per player

## LeagueDashTeamStats

League-wide team statistics dashboard.

- **Result sets**: `LeagueDashTeamStats`
- **Parameters**: `season` (required), `season_type`, `per_mode`, `measure_type`
- **Key columns**: Full traditional + advanced stat line per team

## LeagueStandingsV3

Current and historical league standings.

- **Result sets**: `Standings`
- **Parameters**: `league_id`, `season`, `season_type`
- **Key columns**: `team_id`, `conference`, `division`, `wins`, `losses`, `win_pct`, `playoff_rank`, `conference_record`, `division_record`, `home`, `road`, `l10`, `current_streak`, `pts_pg`, `opp_pts_pg`

## SynergyPlayTypes

Synergy-tracked play type statistics (isolation, pick and roll, post-up, etc.).

- **Result sets**: `SynergyPlayType`
- **Parameters**: `season` (required), `play_type`, `player_or_team`, `type_grouping`
- **Key columns**: `play_type`, `gp`, `poss`, `poss_pct`, `ppp`, `fg_pct`, `efg_pct`, `score_pct`

## LeagueSeasonMatchups

Season-long player-vs-player defensive matchup data.

- **Result sets**: `SeasonMatchups`
- **Parameters**: `season` (required), various player/team filters
- **Key columns**: `off_player_id`, `def_player_id`, `gp`, `matchup_min`, `partial_poss`, `matchup_fgm`, `matchup_fga`, `matchup_fg_pct`

## LeagueDashLineups

Lineup combination statistics.

- **Result sets**: `Lineups`
- **Parameters**: `season` (required), `group_quantity` (2-5 players)
- **Key columns**: `group_set`, `gp`, `min`, `plus_minus`, various per-mode stats

## LeagueLeaders

Season leaders across all statistical categories.

- **Result sets**: `LeagueLeaders`
- **Parameters**: `season` (required), `stat_category`, `per_mode`

## AllTimeLeadersGrids

All-time career and season statistical leaders.

- **Result sets**: Multiple leader grids (points, rebounds, assists, etc.)
