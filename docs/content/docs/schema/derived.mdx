# Derived Aggregation Tables

Derived tables are pre-computed aggregations that speed up common analytical patterns. They are rebuilt during each pipeline run.

## Player Aggregations

### derived_player_career
Career totals and averages for every player across regular season and playoffs.

**Grain**: One row per player per season type.
**Key columns**: player_id, season_type, gp, gs, min_total, pts_total, reb_total, ast_total, ppg, rpg, apg

### derived_player_season
Season-level player statistics with per-game and total modes.

**Grain**: One row per player per season per season type.
**Key columns**: player_id, season_year, gp, ppg, rpg, apg, spg, bpg, fg_pct, fg3_pct, ft_pct, ts_pct

### derived_player_rolling
Rolling averages (5-game, 10-game, 20-game windows) for trend analysis.

**Grain**: One row per player per game.
**Key columns**: player_id, game_id, pts_5g, pts_10g, pts_20g, reb_5g, ast_5g

### derived_player_splits
Player performance splits by home/away, back-to-back, opponent, month, and day of week.

**Grain**: One row per player per split category per value.

### derived_player_estimated
Estimated advanced metrics derived from box score data.

**Grain**: One row per player per season.
**Key columns**: e_off_rating, e_def_rating, e_net_rating, e_usg_pct, e_pace

## Team Aggregations

### derived_team_season
Season-level team statistics.

**Grain**: One row per team per season per season type.
**Key columns**: team_id, season_year, gp, w, l, w_pct, pts_pg, opp_pts_pg, diff_pts_pg

### derived_team_year_by_year
Historical year-by-year team statistics.

**Grain**: One row per team per year.

### derived_team_estimated
Estimated advanced metrics for teams.

**Grain**: One row per team per season.

## League Aggregations

### derived_league_leaders
Season statistical category leaders.

**Grain**: One row per player per stat category per season.
**Key columns**: player_id, season_year, stat_category, stat_value, rank

### derived_league_player_stats
League-wide player dashboard statistics.

**Grain**: One row per player per season per measure type.

### derived_league_team_stats
League-wide team dashboard statistics.

**Grain**: One row per team per season per measure type.

### derived_league_shot_zones
League-wide shot zone efficiency averages.

**Grain**: One row per zone combination per season.
**Key columns**: shot_zone_basic, shot_zone_area, shot_zone_range, fga, fgm, fg_pct

### derived_all_time_leaders
All-time career and season records across statistical categories.

**Grain**: One row per player per stat category.

## Draft Aggregations

### derived_draft_combine
Combined draft combine results with measurements and drill results.

**Grain**: One row per player per combine year.

### derived_player_awards
Aggregated awards and honors per player.

**Grain**: One row per player per award type per season.
